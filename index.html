Intuitive Summary

As self-driving cars become more prevalent, establishing accurate human trust is critical. CW-Net introduces a novel approach to explainable deep learning that transparently communicates the vehicle's decision-making process. By providing clear visual explanations, CW-Net directly improves the fidelity of human mental models, allowing users to accurately predict whether the autonomous system will stop, go, or yield in complex traffic scenarios.
